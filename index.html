Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

Wonder Radio (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Wonder Radio mobile application (“App”) handles information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

Wonder Radio is designed to provide simple access to free radio stations from around the world. We do not require users to create an account.

The App may collect limited technical information automatically, including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App performance and crash data
  • Basic usage analytics
  • Advertising identifiers (if ads are enabled)

This information helps improve app stability and user experience.

Radio Station Content

Wonder Radio streams publicly available radio stations provided by third-party radio directories and broadcasters. We do not host or own the radio streams.

Favorites and Local Storage

The App may store user preferences locally on your device, including:

  • Favorite radio stations

This information is stored only on your device unless otherwise stated.

Advertising

Wonder Radio may display advertisements through third-party advertising providers such as Google AdMob.

These services may collect:

  • Device identifiers

This helps provide relevant advertisements and supports the free availability of the App.

Wonder Radio may use third-party services including:
